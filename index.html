What is Marth Club?

MARTH Club is a community of artists and learners who create art using code.
We believe in the following principles:

  • Community Support: We learn together. We create together. We help each other to learn and create.
  • New-kid Love: We all start as beginners, and we never forget that. We strive to help others to grow by sharing our knowledge freely.
  • No Code-snobs: When someone asks for help, we meet them at their level. We understand that everyone learns differently and that we are all at different levels of coding expertise.
  • Open Source: We share our creations openly so that others might learn from our work and build upon it.
